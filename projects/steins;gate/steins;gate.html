Steins;Gate

Key Information

Genre: Science Fiction, Thriller
Studio: White Fox
Episode Count: 26
Released: 2011

Plot

Set in 2010 in Akihabara, Tokyo, Steins;Gate follows Rintaro Okabe, a self-proclaimed "mad scientist" and his friends who accidentally discover a method of sending text messages to the past using a modified microwave oven. They soon learn that these "D-mails" can alter the present, leading them down a dangerous path of manipulating time and facing unforeseen and often tragic consequences. The narrative explores complex themes of causality, parallel worlds, and the weight of choice as Okabe struggles to undo the changes they've made and save his loved ones from predetermined fates across different timelines ("worldlines").
