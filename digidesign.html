Debian Website Redesign

I made this redesign of the Debian home page with the goal of giving it a modern look. I added a feature for light/dark mode as I believe in giving users the option to choose in how they want their screens to look. Furthermore, I focused on making the page appealing to the new users and make them stay on the page long enough so they can learn about Debian OS and the Debian community.

The goal of this redesign is to make the website as clean and simplistic as possible with a dark theme. Multiple elements of the page are enlarged and some removed/repositioned to make the webpage easier to navigate through with the least amount of mental effort for the users. The home page is divided into 11 parts for ease of design, but the vision is of a single scrollable webpage just like the original site.
